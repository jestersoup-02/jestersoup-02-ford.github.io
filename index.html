Short Biography

Stanford Pines is known as the author of the journals and the twin brother of Stanley Pines in the cartoon Gravity Falls. He's a scientist who studied anomolies in Gravity Falls, Oregon and kept his findings in journals. During that time, he was working with his partner and college friend, Fiddleford McGucket, as well as his "muse", Bill Cipher, to build a portal. This, however, went askew, and he was stuck in the portal for thirty years, until Stan restarted it, and brought him back. His first official appearance in the show is in season two, episode eleven "Not What He Seems". He's introduced as he comes back throught the portal.
